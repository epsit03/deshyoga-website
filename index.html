DESHYOGA CHARITABLE TRUST

We are a non-profit organisation established in 2019 with a mission to promote yoga through scientific methods. The essence of Deshyoga is to explore ancient yogic scriptures and practice their principles and techniques. Our vision is to harmoniously blend ancient wisdom with modern scientific knowledge, extending the benefits of physical, mental, and spiritual well-being to the wider public. We invite everyone to embrace our holistic approach aimed at achieving complete satisfaction and empowering individuals with physical, mental, and spiritual growth. Join us on this transformative journey, where yoga becomes a catalyst for positive change and a path towards profound well-being!
